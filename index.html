A little bit about PaperBatVG and his YouTube Career.

PaperBatVG is a Canadian video maker who started out on YouTube and became famous rather quickly. He is known for his Minecraft, Terraria, Penumbra, Sonic Adventure 2, Mother 3 and many other let's Plays. He is also known for making Minecraft Movie's. He made elven of them, some including other YouTuber's or friends of his. His catch phrases are "Hello, Champs and Champettes it's your boy PBAT", and "Like a bossaru." He usually goes by the name PBat by his subscribers. It's a shortened version of PaperBatVG. He calls his subscribers Champs and Champettes. They are loyal to him and he is loyal back bringing great content to his channel. He currently has 453,293 subscribers. PBat's real name is Dion Anderson. He is very recognizable sporting his famous and distinct afro. PBat is a good influence on his subscriber base since he discourages his "Champs and Champettes" to not smoke, do drugs, or drink alcohol till you are the proper age and not to use guns. He also got a partnership with Machinima which helped bring in new subscribers and fans to his channel.
